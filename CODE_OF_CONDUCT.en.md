# Code of Conduct

[中文](CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md) | [English](CODE_OF_CONDUCT.en.md)

This project aims to maintain a direct, professional, and fact-based collaborative environment.

## Expected Behavior

- Discuss issues, not individuals
- Support conclusions with reproducible information
- Respect contributors of all experience levels
- Accept constructive feedback and revise positions based on evidence

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Personal attacks, public shaming, or harassment
- Deliberate trolling, spamming, or provocation
- Intentionally concealing the impact of high-risk changes
- Posting content unrelated to the project, including advertising, political messaging, or hate speech

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, lock, or remove content that clearly disrupts collaboration. Individuals who engage in serious or repeated violations may be restricted from participating in the repository.
